About Me

My name is Det Sorthepharack. I'm from Minneapolis, MN. My interest in learning coding and web development came from wanting to create a point of sale system for restaurants and retail businesses. One of my goals as a developer is to help small businesses stay up to date with new and improved systems.

Five years from now I hope to have a fulfilling career in web development, accomplishing two things: creating my own app, and using my work to help others live efficient and easy lives.
